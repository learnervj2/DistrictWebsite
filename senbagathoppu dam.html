Senbagathoppu Dam

Across Padvett Ar which is a tributary of Kamandala River that empties into Cheyyar River . senbagathoppu dam.Around this dam natural scene of forest is attractive during rainy season from August to December.Incredible tourist spot for all and nearer to Vellore and Tiruvannamalai. Not far away from Chennai.One cannot see such scene around Chennai with forest and hills.One need not claim hills as Pechipparai and Papanasam dams respectively in KanayaKumar and Tirunelveli Dits.-M.GANDHI

Location: Senbagathoppu, polur Taluk, Tiruvannamalai District, Tamil Nadu

Construction began: 2000

Construction cost: 40.00 crores INR

Impound: Kamandala River

Spillway: 62

Type of dam: Gravity dam

How to Reach:

By Air

Nearest Air Port is Chennai which around 189 Kilometers away from Tiruvannamalai.

By Train

Nearest Railway Station is Tiruvannamalai. This Station is well connected between Vellore and Tirupatthi. This station is going to be link with Chennai very soon.

By Road

From Tiruvannamalai the Senbagathppu Dam is well connected by Road towards vellor to thiruvannamlai, which is 30 Kilometers away from polur.
